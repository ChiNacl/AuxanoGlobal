About the Ministry:

Several years ago, God spoke to our man of God, then a student at the University of Abuja to teach in love the realities of the Kingdom for the purpose making the Gospel make sense to ordinary everyday people with questions & concerns. He was led to begin teaching everyone that was in his sphere, he started from a friend's room before moving to other facilities within the campus environs. In the midst of the chaos of 2020, the Lord led His servant to move into the city of Abuja to start a new work. Not having anyone in the city but with simple obedience to the voice of God he moved to start up the work. Today, AUXANO GLOBAL exist to make the Gospel make sense to ordinary everyday people with questions & concerns by teaching in love the realities of the Kingdom, to bring the healing power of God to the peoples of the world, to communicate Kingdom culture to ordinary everyday people through the Ministry of the Word and the Spirit.

Pastor Noble Rowland

Pastor Noble Rowland is the Lead Pastor and President of AUXANO GLOBAL. He is called to the office of the teacher, with thousands of lives touched around the world in over 4 continents, his teaching Ministry brings to the hearer the reality of that which is being taught. He is graced with a unique Healing Ministry after the Lord spoke to him, "Heal the sick in the world! Take My healing power to the peoples of the world". He is the convener of several city-wide meetings such as AUXANO (weekly teaching meeting), ATMOSPHERE OF THE SPIRIT (a special meeting for the demonstration of the Presence, Character & Gifts of the Spirit), HEALING SCHOOL (a special healing session) and several other expressions of the Ministry.

Our Vision:

To see the church come to full stature, the fullness of Christ. (Eph 4:13)

Our mission:

To teach in love the realities of the Kingdom. (Eph 4:15)

Our purpose:

To make the Gospel make sense to ordinary everyday people with questions and concerns. (Matt 28:18-20)

Our core values:

Spirituality, Excellence, Enthusiasm, Love

Our belief:

Our beliefs has its source in the Bible and is in total agreement with the principles of the doctrines of Christ.

  • We believe that the Bible contains the inspired and infallible Word of God. (2 Timothy 3:16, 2 Peter 1:20-21).
  • We believe that there is only one God eternally existent in three persons; God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Ghost. (Ephesians 4:4-6, Gen. 1:26, Matthew 3:16-17, Matthew 28:19).
  • We believe in the deity of Christ. (John 1:1-4; John 10:30; Hebrews 1:1-5).
    • He was born of a virgin (Matthew 1:18-25; Luke 1:26-35)
    • He was conceived of the Holy Ghost (Matthew 1:18-25, Luke 1:30-35)
    • He died (John 19:30-35)
    • He rose again from the dead bodily (John 20:25-29, Luke 24:36-40)
    • He ascended into heaven bodily (1 Thessalonians 4:16-17; Matthew 29-30)
  • We believe in the rapture of the church and the Second coming of Christ. (1 Thessalonians 4:16-17; 1 Corinthians 15:52; Matthew 24:31; Matthew 24:29-30; Revelation 1:7; Revelation 19:11-16)
  • We believe that the only means of being cleansed from sin is repentance and faith in the precious blood of Jesus (Acts 3:19, Luke 24:47; Ephesians 1:7).
  • We believe regeneration by the power of the Holy Spirit through the Word of God is essential for personal salvation (Titus 2:5, John 3:3-5, Ephesians 5:25-27).
  • We believe that the redemptive work of Christ on the cross provides divine healing for the body, salvation for the soul of everyone that believes. (1 Peter 2:24; Acts 3:16, Acts 9:32 -35).
  • We believe that when an person receives Salvation through the regeneration by the Holy Ghost, he receives divine enablement for Christian service and witness. (Acts 1:8; 2:4; 3:1-26; 4:5–12).
  • We believe in the sanctifying power of the Holy Ghost. (Romans 15:16; 1 Corinthians 6:11).
  • We believe in the final resurrection of both the saved and the lost; the former to eternal life and the latter to eternal judgement. (Revelations 20: 11-15; 1 Corinthians 15: 12–23)
