As a partner with the Ministry in teaching in love the realities of the Kingdom, to see the Church come to full stature (the fullness of Christ). Here are a few things you should know;

WHO IS A PARTNER?

A partner is that one person that understands and aligns himself/herself with the vision and mission of Auxano Global, and is committed to giving regularly and consistently towards the work of Ministry, while praying for the success of the work.

PARTNERSHIP BENEFITS:

  1. As a partner, you affirm that you are "one with the Lord" in every way including building His Church, which is your declaration of faith.
  2. One of the greatest blessing of partnership is knowing that through your continual giving and prayer, lives are being changed and transformed by the teaching of the realities of the Kingdom in love, bringing them to the fullness of Christ.
  3. As you give as a partner, God will make all grace abound towards you, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good work. (2 Cor 9:8).
Note: The Ministry team would reach out to you consistently with updates from the Ministry. Also, top partners would have exclusive time out with the man of God periodically.
